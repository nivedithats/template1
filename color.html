Defining Color values

  1. a COLORKEYWORD - Like "red" "green" "blue","yellow"
  2. a HEXAvalue - like "#000" "#fff" ,"#c5c5c5"
  3. a RGBAvalue - like (red,green,blue,alpha)"rgb(255,100,100,1)"
